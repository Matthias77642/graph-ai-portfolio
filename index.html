About me

Senior Research Fellow at the University of Zurich and Visiting Professor at the Universities of Bristol and Cape Town. My research focuses on large cohort studies, pragmatic trials, and meta‑analyses in infectious diseases and cancer. Contributions include work with the Swiss HIV Cohort Study, international HIV collaborations (ART Cohort Collaboration, IeDEA), the WHO Ebola ring vaccination trial, and the Swiss National Cohort.

  • Current roles: Senior Research Fellow (University of Zurich); Visiting Professor (Bristol; Cape Town).
  • Interests: Meta‑analysis, observational methods, modelling, reporting guidelines (STROBE, CONSORT), research‑on‑research.
  • Past leadership: Professor of Epidemiology & Public Health (University of Bern); President, Swiss National Science Foundation Research Council.
