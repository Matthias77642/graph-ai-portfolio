About me

Senior Research Fellow at the University of Zurich and Visiting Professor at the Universities of Bristol and Cape Town. My research focuses on large cohort studies, pragmatic trials, and meta‑analyses in infectious diseases and cancer. Contributions include work with the Swiss HIV Cohort Study, international HIV collaborations (ART Cohort Collaboration, IeDEA), the WHO Ebola ring vaccination trial, and the Swiss National Cohort.

  • Current roles: Senior Research Fellow (University of Zurich); Visiting Professor (Bristol; Cape Town).
  • Interests: Meta‑analysis, observational methods, modelling, reporting guidelines (STROBE, CONSORT), and research‑on‑research.
  • Past leadership: Professor of Epidemiology & Public Health (University of Bern); President of the Research Council (Swiss National Science Foundation).

About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8‑week live cohort program that focuses on no‑code AI tools for responsible task automation. The bootcamp features weekly live workshops, help sessions, a private community, personalized feedback, capstone coaching, a shareable certificate, and lifetime access to a tutorial library.

Module 1 — Websites, Documents & Presentations

  • How LLMs are trained; overview of providers
  • Generate Markdown, LaTeX, and HTML
  • Build simple sites with no‑code tools
  • Markdown for structured research docs
  • AI presentation tools for science comms

Module 2 — AI‑Automated Data Analysis

  • Quick analysis with code interpreters
  • Automated dashboards
  • Dataset processing & transformation
  • Batch file operations
  • Automated reports & visualizations

Module 3 — GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

  • Literature reviews with AI tools
  • Text classification & sentiment analysis
  • Knowledge maps & summarization flows

Module 4 — Custom GPTs & Chatbots

  • LLM chat architecture & RAG
  • Custom GPTs for use‑cases
  • Interactive chatbots; knowledge bases

Module 5 — AI for Task Automation

  • Agent‑based systems
  • Email and browser automations
  • Multi‑step workflow design

Module 6 — GenAI for Communication & Outreach

  • Research summaries for varied audiences
  • Scientific figures & diagrams with AI
  • Video abstracts & content scheduling

Module 7 — Capstone Project & Showcase

  • Progress checks & peer feedback
  • Final presentation & public Q&A

Bootcamp features

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Private community & personalized feedback
  • Capstone coaching & showcase
  • Shareable certificate
  • Lifetime tutorial library access

Grading rubric

  • Online quizzes — 15%
  • Workshop assignments — 35%
  • Attendance & participation — 10%
  • Final project — 40%

Passing grade: 80%. Attend ≥ 5 workshops for full attendance credit.
